Sweet Corn

Ingredients

  • 3 cup frozen sweet corn
  • 2 teaspoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • salt as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes

Instructions

  1. Place frozen sweet corn in water to thaw.
  2. Discard the water once the corn is fully thawed.
  3. Now place sweet corn in a mixing bowl.
  4. Add red chilli powder, chaat masala, cumin powder, salt and coriander leaves
  5. Now add butter and squeeze lime juice.
  6. Mix with a spoon until well combined.
  7. Enjoy spicy masala corn with a cup of tea or on its own.
